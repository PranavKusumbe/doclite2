About DocLite

Your trusted companion for secure, client-side document processing.

Our Story

DocLite was born from a simple idea: powerful tools shouldn't be complicated or require you to give up your data. In a world where online privacy is increasingly scarce, we wanted to create a toolbox where users could perform tasks on their documents and images with complete peace of mind. That's why we committed to a 100% client-side processing model. When you use DocLite, your files are never uploaded to a server. All the magic happens right in your browser.

Our Philosophy

Privacy First

We believe your files are your business. We don't track, store, or analyze your data.

Simplicity

Our tools are designed to be intuitive and easy to use, regardless of your technical expertise.

Performance

By leveraging your browser's capabilities, we offer a fast and responsive experience without server lag.

What We Offer

DocLite provides a wide range of tools across several categories:

  • PDF Tools: Merge, split, compress, and convert PDFs.
  • Image Tools: Compress, resize, crop, and convert images.
  • Text Tools: Word counting, case conversion, and more.
  • Developer Tools: Formatters, minifiers, and generators.

We are constantly working to improve our existing tools and add new ones to make DocLite the only online toolbox you'll ever need. Thank you for choosing DocLite.
