Frequently Asked Questions

How do I merge PDF files online?

Upload multiple PDF files using the upload area. They will appear in a list where you can drag and drop to reorder them. Click "Merge PDFs" to combine them into a single document. The merged PDF will be ready to download instantly.

Can I change the order of PDFs before merging?

Yes! Simply drag and drop the files in the list to reorder them. The PDFs will be merged in the order shown in the list, from top to bottom.

Is there a limit on file size or number of PDFs?

Since all processing happens in your browser, the limit depends on your device's memory. Generally, you can merge dozens of PDFs totaling several hundred MB. Larger files may take longer to process.

Are my PDFs uploaded to a server?

No! All PDF merging happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your files never leave your computer, ensuring complete privacy and security. This also means you can use the tool offline.

Can I merge password-protected PDFs?

Currently, password-protected PDFs cannot be merged. Please remove the password protection first using our "Unlock PDF" tool, then merge the files.
