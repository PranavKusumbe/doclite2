Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert PDF to JPG online free?

Simply upload your PDF file, choose the quality settings, and click convert. All pages will be extracted as separate JPG images that you can download individually or as a ZIP file.

Can I convert multiple PDFs at once?

Yes! Upload multiple PDF files and they will all be processed. Each PDF's pages will be converted to JPG images.

What's the best DPI setting?

For screen viewing, 72 or 150 DPI works well. For printing or high-quality needs, use 300 DPI. Higher DPI creates larger file sizes.
