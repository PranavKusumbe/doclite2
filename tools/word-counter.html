Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the word counter?

Our word counter uses industry-standard algorithms to count words, matching the methods used by major word processors like Microsoft Word and Google Docs.

Does it count hyphenated words correctly?

Yes! Hyphenated words are counted as single words, which is the standard practice in most writing contexts.

How is reading time calculated?

Reading time is calculated based on an average reading speed of 200-250 words per minute, which is typical for adult readers.
