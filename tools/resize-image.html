Frequently Asked Questions

How do I resize an image online?

Upload your image, enter the desired width and height, check "Maintain aspect ratio" if needed, and click Resize. The tool will process your image instantly.

What does "Maintain aspect ratio" mean?

When enabled, the tool automatically adjusts height based on width (or vice versa) to prevent distortion. Disable it to set exact custom dimensions.

Can I resize multiple images at once?

Yes! Upload multiple images and they'll all be resized to the same dimensions. Download individually or as a ZIP file.
